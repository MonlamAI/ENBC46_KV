The Regent of Padma Regent of Padma, Do Ngak Lingpa, To you I pray with unshakeable, single-pointed devotion! In this life, the next, and the bardo state, at all times inspire me with your blessings, Pacify all impediments, and bestow the supreme and ordinary accomplishments! Khyentse Wangpo Pema Ösal Do Ngak Lingpa wrote this for the vajra holder and border official Wangchuk Senge, who made a wholehearted request from afar for a prayer that could be recited. May it bring virtue!
